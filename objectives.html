Objectives

This usability test is to develop recommendations to guide the product team toward improve usability and user satisfaction in the design of a new app. They will use the improved features to increase business production and support user efficiency

User experience objectives:

  • Understand the current features of competitive note-taking apps during onboarding and basic note-taking functions.
  • Determine what constitutes user satisfaction.
  • Survey new user pain points of note-taking apps.
  • Prioritize user problems from highest to lowest.

Business objectives:

  • Define user needs that provide note-taking apps with a competitive edge.
  • Define usability considerations for the development of a note-taking app prototype.
  • Provide recommendations on how to align usability features with Calm Technology principles.
